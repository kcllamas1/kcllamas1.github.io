A color that I feel is really part of my culture is hot pink. I am a mexican-american and this color's vibrancy
reflects the unapologetic and authentic mindset we approach life with. I personally really like the font that
I chose for this.I am not one that resorts to simplistic characteristics which is why I chose this one.
The serifs always add a certain flair and it feels interesting to read. It kind of feels like you have the freedom
to read it rather than being forced to. In a way.
In this page, we focused on making the color readable for every person. We put out color palettes through a webside
that measures contrast. Unfortunatly my original palette did not have enough contrast with the white background
This made my process much longer as I had to find palettes and adjust them, then test their contrast again and again.
I tested maybe 4-5 palettes. This ended up being something I am not completely happy with but had to settle for
in order to follow the contrast requirements for the text.
Creating a cohesive design system was just much harder than I though it would be. Considering so many factors
was incredibly difficult. However, little by little I am learning just what i need to take into
consideration when i am making a site. I know now that i need to be mindful of the text's readability.
